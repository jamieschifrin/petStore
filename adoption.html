We here at The House of Exotics are committed to preserving biodiversity both locally and abroad.
In an attempt to support local organizations, we are proud to introduce our newest partnership with an amazing local group: Raptors Inc.


Serving the tri-state area since the late 1970’s, Raptor Inc. is dedicated to the protection, preservation, and rehabilitation of local Raptors. They hold monthly meet and greets with Avion’s in their care. For more information please click the link below.

Our Mission

While some people may question the private ownership of Exotic Animals, it has always been our position that caring individuals will always create loving homes for all animals, common place or Exotic. We seek to place unique and inspiring Animals with unique and inspiring People.
